# Research with OpenDT

OpenDT builds on the [OpenDC](https://opendc.org) simulator. This page lists publications related to OpenDT and OpenDC.

## Interested in working with us?

If you are interested in doing a project (e.g., bachelor thesis, master thesis) on OpenDT, please visit the [AtLarge Research website](https://www.atlarge-research.com/new_students.html) for more information.
